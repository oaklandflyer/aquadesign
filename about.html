Our Story

Built by someone who
understands systems.

Aqua Design Collective was founded on a simple idea: that a well-designed aquascape should feel effortless to live with, and extraordinary to look at.

Michael Coutinho
Founder, Aqua Design Collective
linkedin.com/in/mcoutinho1

A decade working at the intersection of innovation and complex systems — now applied to the living art of aquascaping.

Michael Coutinho spent nearly ten years on the innovation side of healthcare — working with nonprofits, navigating federal grant programs, and ultimately investing in early-stage digital health companies at UPMC Enterprises. That career taught him one enduring lesson: the best outcomes come when you design systems that are both precise and adaptive.

Aqua Design Collective is that same thinking applied to something he has loved since long before the boardroom — planted aquariums. Each tank is a living system with its own chemistry, biology, and aesthetic logic. Getting it right requires patience, an eye for detail, and genuine care for how all the parts interact. Michael brings all of that to every build.

Based in Pittsburgh, he works directly with every client — from the first conversation through ongoing maintenance — because he believes the relationship between a space and its aquascape deserves that kind of attention.

What Drives the Work
🎯

Precision

Every element — water chemistry, hardscape placement, plant selection — is considered intentionally, not assembled by default.

🌱

Living Systems

An aquascape is not static art. It grows, shifts, and responds. Michael designs builds that stay beautiful as they mature.

🤝

Direct Relationship

You work with Michael, not a team of subcontractors. He handles the consultation, the build, and the ongoing care himself.
